Using BetterAt to augment online and distance learning

We've spend the last 5 years researching learning & digital media

It's expected that more than forty percent of the workforce in the United States will be millennials. We've spent the past five years meticulously researching people (digital natives and millennials) learning new skills and improving themselves. Then we built a platform tailor made for learning in the digital age.
