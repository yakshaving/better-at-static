Frequently Asked Questions

What is an "Interest Network"?

Interest networks are ways to organize your life around the interests you have -- whether it's the people and groups, or the content and experiences that you have around a particular interest.

Why would I (or my students / employees) need an "interest network"?

Interest networks are ways for the best and relevant content to be curated and shared with a group of people. Unlike social networks, Interest networks are laser focused, intent based, and purpose oriented. This is a meaningful difference.

What is a BetterAt plan?

Plans are curated activities that you or your employees can use to get better at their interest. Plans are dynamic, allow for feedback, and collaboration with both the plan author and others who are using the same plan.
